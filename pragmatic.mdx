title: "Pragmatic Engineering"
---

### Definition

> Pragmatic Engineering is dealing with things sensibly and realistically in a way that is based on practical rather than theoretical considerations.

## Core Principles

- Focus on the practical rather than the theoretical perfection
- Emphasis on Simplicity and Clarity
- Adaptibility to changing requirements
- Continous Learning and Improvements
- Effective communication within the Team
- Balancing between planning and action
